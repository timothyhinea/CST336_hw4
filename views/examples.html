Smartwatch - EM-Sense is a technique that uses the body’s natural electrical conductivity to detect if a person is touching an electrical or electromechanical device. Doing so allows it to automatically identify the object. The smartwatch therefore could automatically start a timer when the user begins using an electric toothbrush, unlock keyboard without password on laptop, or play the latest news when breakfast is being prepared.

A bizarre example of the Internet of Things is that sensors are implanted in the ears of cows so they are connected and monitored. This helps farmers to keep track of the cows’ health and movements to make sure that the cows are healthy and produce a substantial amount of milk.

Garage Doors - Products such as Gogogate2 allow consumers to turn their garage doors into an IoT device. This allows users to open, close, and monitor the status of their garage door from anywhere in the world.
